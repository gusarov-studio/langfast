What is included in the free plan?

The free plan gets you access to essential LangFast features, 6 LLMs and 1,500 free LLM requests per month. You can have as many private and public prompts as you want.

What is a request?

A request is a call to an LLM provider such as OpenAI or Anthropic. From the playground, evalutaiotns, via the API, or from any forms you build.
